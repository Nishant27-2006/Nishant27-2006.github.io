A4D
Functional latent spaces for affordance reasoning

What Objects Enable, Not What They Are: Functional Latent Spaces for Affordance Reasoning

CoRL 2026 (Under Review)
Siva, R., Bhatt, N. P., Yang, Y., Lee, S., Gadde, N., Ellis, C., Velasquez, A., Wang, Z., Topcu, U.

A4D maps visual observations into a shared functional latent space structured around affordances (e.g., "movable"), enabling robot planning based on task-relevant object functionalities rather than appearance alone. We achieve 94% inference accuracy on existing affordances — over 15% above state-of-the-art — while enabling 100x faster inference.

Deep Learning · Power Systems
AC-OPF
Learning to approximate non-convex optimal power flow

Deep Network Approximation of Non-Convex AC Optimal Power Flow

IEEE CAI, 2025
Gadde, N. et al. · Stanford Sustainability Systems Laboratory (S3L)

Approximates non-convex AC optimal power flow with deep networks, achieving a <1% optimality gap at ~1000x lower solve time than a conventional solver. Feasibility is enforced via SOCP convex relaxations during training, with PCA-compressed grid features for stronger generalization.

Hybrid CNN+SVM Pipeline for ECG Classification on PhysioNet Signals

IEEE ICMLCA, 2025
Gadde, N. et al. · Cardiovascular Fluid Mechanics Laboratory, Georgia Tech
