Vision-Language · Robotics
A4D
Functional latent spaces for affordance reasoning

What Objects Enable, Not What They Are: Functional Latent Spaces for Affordance Reasoning

CoRL 2026 · Under Review
Siva, R., Bhatt, N. P., Yang, Y., Lee, S., Gadde, N., Ellis, C., Velasquez, A., Wang, Z., Topcu, U.

A4D maps visual observations into a shared functional latent space structured around affordances (e.g. "movable"), enabling robot planning based on task-relevant object functionalities rather than appearance alone. It reaches 94% inference accuracy — over 15 points above a GPT-5-class baseline — while delivering 22 ms latency (~100× faster) with isotonic-calibrated uncertainty for fallback.

Learning to approximate non-convex optimal power flow

Deep Network Approximation of Non-Convex AC Optimal Power Flow

IEEE CAI 2025
Gadde, N. et al. · Stanford Sustainability Systems Laboratory (S3L)

Approximates non-convex AC optimal power flow with deep networks, achieving a <1% optimality gap at ~1000× lower solve time than a conventional solver. Feasibility is enforced through SOCP convex relaxations during training, with PCA-compressed grid features for stronger generalization.
